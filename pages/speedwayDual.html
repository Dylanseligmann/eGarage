Speedway 5 Dual

U$S $1,700.00

The fifth generation of the Speedway electric scooter is here. With Dual motors, Dual air spring suspensions, a powerful battery and a completely reworked frame, the Speedway 5 is here to conquer the urban landscape with style!

The Urban Environment can be a very volatile place, with many different obstacles and hills. Therefore the electric scooter must have a powerful motor to survive the increasingly tougher environment. That is why the Speedway 5 is the first Speedway Electric Scooter to come with Dual motors that can produce a mind staggering output of MAX 3600W. The power of the Speedway 5 de facto makes the struggle of urban riding a thing of the past!

The huge 60V 23.4 AH battery in the Speedway 5 can deliver an impressive range of up to 80 km, giving you the opportunity to ride in the urban environment without ever having to think about recharging.

The Speedway 5 comes with 10 inch & 70mm wide tubeless tires providing you with great comfort and traction on the road. The tubeless design greatly reduces the risk of punctures and allows for easy repairs when the tire has been damaged. Thanks to the modular wheel design, the tires can be easily changed and maintained without replacing the motor in the event of a breakdown.
